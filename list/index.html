How make a pancakes

Ingredients
Eggs, milk, flour, baking soda, vainilla, sugar.
Mix
Mix all the ingredients together until they are homogeneous.
Cook
Pour the mixture into a hot pan and wait for bubbles to appear, then turn the pancake over, wait a minute and remove it from the pan. Repeat this until the mixture is gone.
Eat
Serve the pancakes on a plate and add butter, honey or jam of any kind to your liking.
