Bayonetta Origins: Cereza and the Lost Demon

    Cereza and the Lost Demon suggests a magical and dreamy world that takes ideas from the Brothers Grimm tales. It's like a place where most of the exciting stuff happens in an enchanted forest with magical creatures, witches, and demons.

Main characters:

    •Cereza: She's a young apprentice learning to be a witch. Now, she wants to prove to her teacher that she's good at doing magic.

    •Cheshire/Pinpollo: Pimpollo is Cereza's cat plush toy. Cheshire is the demon who lives inside the toy. He will assist her on this adventure.

The beginning

    Morgana, Cereza's teacher, decides that it's time for the young witch to summon her first demon. She succeeds, but gets scared, causing the contract not to be completed. Cereza wants to prove to Morgana that she can do it, so she decides to go into the forest that her teacher warned her about many times.

    In the forest, she faces her first enemies. The scared girl summons a demon to defend herself, but she forgets to give it a body. In its desperation, the demon enters the stuffed cat toy that the girl had with her.
