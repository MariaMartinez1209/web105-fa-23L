The pros of the powers

Shunsuke Otosaka

Ability to time travel, allowing corrections to past events.

Yuu Otosaka

Can possess bodies and use their abilities.

Misa Kurobane

Possesses the power of Pyrokinesis while inhabiting Yusa Nishimori's body, allowing her to generate and manipulate fire.

Takehito Kumagami

Has the ability to locate anyone with a special ability.

Nao Tomori

Can become invisible to one person at a time, useful for infiltration.

Jojiro Takajo

Superhuman speed, advantageous in combat and evasion.

The cons of the powers

Shunsuke Otosaka

Each time jump results in the gradual loss of his sight. When completely blind, he can no longer use his temporal power.

Yuu Otosaka

When possessing someone with powers, he permanently steals those powers, which could have ethical and personal implications.

Misa Kurobane

Us her power may have moral and ethical implications, and constant threats with fire can lead to conflicts.

Takehito Kumagami

Although initially believed to require soaking himself in water to use his ability, it is later revealed that he only did it to conceal his identity. The extent and limitations of his ability may not be fully known.

Nao Tomori

Limited to one target at a time, and invisibility doesn't work against all powers.

Jojiro Takajo

Challenges in controlling speed and direction.
